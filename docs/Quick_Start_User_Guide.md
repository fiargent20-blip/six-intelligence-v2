# Quick Start User Guide

## Welcome to Scribe (V2)
Scribe is a high-density intelligence engine designed to instantly transcribe, paraphrase, and extract strategic tasks perfectly out of long-form corporate meetings.

## 1. Capturing a Live Meeting
1. From your **Dashboard**, tap the massive **Start Capture** panel.
2. Grant microphone permissions natively if prompted.
3. Scribe intelligently chops your raw audio into infinite slices natively in real time. **Every 15 seconds**, you will see a deeply condensed paraphrased timeline update instantly in the right column!
4. When your meeting concludes, firmly click **Stop Stream**. 
5. Scribe will instantly bypass massive audio payload processing entirely, instantly transferring your live text points natively to Gemini 3.0 to formulate your Strategy Summary, Core Objectives, and Actions.

## 2. Ingesting Offline Audio 
*(Use this method exclusively if you forgot to start Scribe during your live meeting and recorded it via an external device, like an iPhone).*

1. From the **Dashboard**, click **Upload Media**.
2. Click or drag an `.m4a`, `.mp3`, `.wav`, or `.webm` file directly into the drop zone.
3. Because some meetings exceed 100MB in total size, Scribe safely negotiates a custom, multi-part 8MB secure tunnel to Google Cloud natively. 
4. The Progress Bar will climb mathematically to 100%. Once done, wait 10-20 seconds for the backend inference servers natively to execute their timeline. Your Intelligence Board will spawn dynamically onto the screen with your complete Paraphrased Transcript!

## 3. Working with Your Data
- All data is fundamentally stored mathematically **only on your device** inside an offline Database! No other user has access to it natively.
- Tap **"Delete"** from your Dashboard permanently to sever the intelligence map definitively locally.
- Tap **"Export PDF"** mathematically straight from your finalized note pipeline to natively download corporate briefs!
